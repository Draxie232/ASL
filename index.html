WISHLIST ON STEAM

A 400-YEAR-OLD CURSE

In the heart of Kashi, an ancient fort stands frozen in time, cursed by a saint centuries ago. Lured by legend, four friends breach its walls, only to find that the curse is real. Death is not an escape. As the lone survivor, you must navigate the suffocating darkness, guided by flickering diyas and haunted by whispers of the past. Your only way out is to attain Moksha—a salvation that demands the ultimate sacrifice.

SURVIVE THE NIGHTMARE

Flickering Diyas

Light is your sanctuary and your most precious resource. Use sacred diyas to illuminate cryptic clues, ward off unseen horrors, and find your way through the oppressive dark. But be warned—the oil won't last forever.

Whispered Mantras

Sound is your weapon. Decipher and recite ancient mantras to solve esoteric puzzles, pacify lingering spirits, and protect yourself from the wrath of Kaal Bhairav. Every chant could be your last.

Cryptic Puzzles

The fort's history is written in its walls. Solve logic challenges, decode ancient scripts, and navigate riddles rooted in Indian mythology. Your intellect is your sharpest tool against a divine, ancient power.

FACE THE GAZE OF KAAL BHAIRAV

Kashi, the city of light, holds deep shadows. The cursed fort is a realm outside of time, a personal hell where Kaal Bhairav, the fierce aspect of Shiva, is the ultimate guardian. He is not a monster to be fought, but a divine wrath to be understood. His presence warps reality, and every shadow could be his judgment.
